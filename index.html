ABOUT.

I am Shirley Chen, a freshman at Carnegie Mellon University studying architecture as major and music as minor. I have lots of interests, including drawing, painting, singing, playing drum sets and piano, running, traveling, and cooking. I come from Guangzhou, China and went to high school in Dublin, California. I am so fortunate to study in different countries and these unique experiences in each culture give me various perspectives to look at this world. I wish to bring my unique perspective to my design. My dream is to travel to poor areas in Africa, living and communicating with local residences for a while and developing efficient architectural designs to solve major issues. I love sunshine, summer breeze, cats, smile, friends and my lovely family.
